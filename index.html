About Our NCC Unit

Our Mission

To nurture disciplined and motivated citizens who are physically fit, mentally robust, and ready to take up challenges with confidence.

Values We Uphold

  • Discipline & responsibility
  • Teamwork & leadership
  • Community service
  • Respect for the uniform

Training Focus

Drill, weapon training, adventure sports, first aid, map reading, disaster response, and civic action projects conducted year-round.
